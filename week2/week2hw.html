About

Lacrosse is a team sport played with a stick that has a mini net on it and a lacrosse ball. Players use the head of a lacrosse stick to catch, throw, and shoot the ball throughout the game.

Rules

Lacrosse has various rules regarding player positions, scoring, fouls, and game duration. The game is typically played with ten players on each team, including a goalie. Players must abide by rules involving stick and body contact, offsides, and other aspects of gameplay.
